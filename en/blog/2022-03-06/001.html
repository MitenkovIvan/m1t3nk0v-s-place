Crosser Bot gets hacked


March 6, 2022, 16:15 MSK

Today many Telegram channels posted the same political post about Russian invasion of Ukraine. It starts with the following words:

Translation: We will have to delete this message, and some of us will even be fired or imprisoned, but we cannot take it anymore.

It looks like someone hacked a bot called Crosser Bot and spread political posts through it in all the channels that use it. Crosser Bot team has already confirmed the hack and advised channel administrators to manually delete messages from it.

Digital Review reports that at least 3,6K channels suffered from hack. The post reached more than a million views, even given that two thirds of the posts have already been deleted.

Stay tuned and take care of yourself!
