Winamp. It really SUCKS the llama's ass


March 17, 2022, 13:40 MSK
Last updated: 16:30 MSK

Shame on you, Radionomy, for NOT releasing any new Winamp versions, NOT updating about Winamp itself and instead announcing just another NFT bullshit.

Instead of Winamp 6, which obviously will not come out for another million years, I suggest the following alternatives:

  • WACUP - a Winamp 5.666 modification by ex-Winamp developer Darren Owen
  • Webamp - an open-source HTML5 reimplementation of Winamp 2.9
  • foobar2000 - a free audio player by ex-Nullsoft developer Peter Pawlowski
  • Audacious - an open-source audio player with Winamp skin support
  • Qmmp - an open-source audio player written in Qt, also supports Winamp skins (thanks to HSD for suggesting)
  • AIMP - why not? :-)
  • any version of original Winamp up to 5.666
