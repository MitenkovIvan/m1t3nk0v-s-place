Twitter is NO MORE in Russia!


March 5, 2022, 10:05 MSK

Yesterday there were news that Roskomnadzor started blocking Twitter in Russia.

Roskomnadzor, a Russian government censorship agency, cited Facebook’s "discrimination against Russian state media outlets" as the reason for the ban.

No matter what do we all think about Twitter and its userbase, the block itself is depressing. But as long as there are many VPNs, don't worry.

Just in case you don't like Twitter anyway, there are alternatives too. Mastodon or Pleroma, for example.

Stay tuned and take care of yourself!
